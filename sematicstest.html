What's EdgeDev?

EdgeDev by Layeredge is a powerful and beginner-friendly tech learning initiative designed to empower aspiring developers with real-world skills. The course walks you through the fundamentals of web development, starting from HTML and CSS, and gradually builds up to advanced topics like JavaScript, React, and full-stack development.

What makes EdgeDev unique is its hands-on, project-based approach—turning theory into practice with live assignments, mentor support, and a vibrant peer community. Powered by Layeredge, a forward-thinking platform fostering growth in emerging tech spaces, EdgeDev is more than just a course—it's a gateway to unlocking your potential in the world of tech, completely free and accessible to all.
